This project analyzes the **2024–2025 Tamil Nadu Engineering Seat Matrix** released by the **Tamil Nadu government** to uncover key trends across engineering branches and institutions. The primary goal is to create awareness among students about seat distribution, popular disciplines, and opportunity concentration before the counselling process.

Key insights from the analysis include:

- Dominance of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), reflecting sustained demand for technology-driven careers
- Continued relevance of core branches such as ECE, EEE, and Mechanical Engineering
- Growing presence of new-age programs like Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
- Uneven seat distribution, with a small number of colleges accounting for a large share of total seats
